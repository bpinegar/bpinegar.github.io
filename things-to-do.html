Things to Do

The Cashiers area is spectacularly beautiful and definitely offers something for everyone - from hiking, golfing, shopping, relaxing, eating, and more. We can’t wait for you to experience it!

Hiking

Whitewater Falls
Whitewater Falls is the highest waterfall east of the Rocky Mountains.

Whiteside Mountain
For easy hiking, explore Whiteside Mountain, a two mile easy to moderate loop that follows a path that runs along that Eastern Continental Divide and has some spectacular views of very high cliffs.

Panthertown Valley
Panthertown Valley is a wilderness area that offers numerous hiking trails and waterfalls. More info here.

Hiking at High Hampton
There are 15-miles of well-marked hiking trails on property. Some great options include:

  • Chimney Top Mountain
    Our particular favorite! Elevation 4,618 feet with excellent views in all directions. Allow 2-2.5 hours walking time. Walking distance from the Inn to the top is 1.6 miles. Grade is mild for 2/3 of the way and moderate/steep for the last 1/3.
  • Rock Mountain Trail
    Mild/moderate hike. To reach the summit of Rock Mountain at 4,370 feet, you will need about 1-1.5 hours. The views from the top of the trail are spectacular.

Below are a couple of websites with more information on these hikes, plus some others that you can try as well!
