Things to Do

Hiking

Whiteside Mountain
Two mile loop with a moderate incline, great overlooks. From main crossroads in Cashiers, go west on Highway 64 for 4.7 miles. Turn left a Whiteside Mountain Road at Whiteside Mountain Trail sign. Follow signs.

Panthertown Valley
Tons of hiking and mountain biking trails. You need a map to navigate these trails. Maps are sold at the Highland Hiker in Cashiers.

High Hampton Inn Hiking Trails
Eight well-marked NC mountain hiking trails in Cashiers lined with hemlock, rhododendron and mountain laurel, offer a variety of skill levels and traverse the 1,400 acres of the High Hampton estate.

Chimney Top
Tough, but short 1.6 mile hike offering fabulous views of Cashiers Valley and the Blue Ridge Mountains. Available to guests staying at High Hampton.
