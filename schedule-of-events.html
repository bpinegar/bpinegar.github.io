Schedule of Events

Friday, October 2nd, 2015

Rehearsal Dinner

  • The Pavilion at High Hampton Inn and Country Club
    1525 US Highway 107 South
    Cashiers, North Carolina 28717
  • 6:00 PM to 9:00PM

To celebrate us not screwing up the ceremony dry run, there will be a Rehearsal Dinner (bar-b-que!!!) for members of the wedding party and our families.

Smart Casual, whatever your interpretation of that is.

Pizza Dinner

  • Slab Town Pizza
    45 Slab Town Road
    Cashiers, North Carolina 28717
  • 7:00 PM to 9:00PM

For those guests not attending the Rehearsal Dinner, please stop by to enjoy good company, pizza and drinks at Slab Town Pizza (the best pizza in Cashiers)!

Attire:
Smart Casual, whatever your interpretation of that is.

Welcome Party

  • Slab Town Pizza
    45 Slab Town Road
    Cashiers, North Carolina 28717
  • 9:00 PM to 11:00PM

Getting into town on Friday? Feel free to stop by and help us kick off the weekend's festivities with a cocktail (or two or three...) and some dessert!

Smart Casual, whatever your interpretation of that is.

Saturday, October 3rd, 2015

Wedding Ceremony

  • Lonesome Valley
    94 Lonesome Valley Road
    Sapphire, North Carolina 28717
  • 5:00 PM to 5:30PM

The ceremony will be held outdoors in the Valley at 5PM, weather permitting. Transportation to the ceremony will be provided from the official wedding hotels: High Hampton Inn & Country Club and Hampton Inn & Suites. Parking will also be available on-site.

Attire:
Suit and tie or a fancy dress - you know, the stuff you usually wear to a wedding. If you want to wear a tux, feel free, but know that you will have to do a James Bond impression at some point during the night.

Things to Keep in Mind:
The ceremony will be held in the grass, therefore ladies might want to consider wearing flats, wedges, or thick heels. It will likely be a little chilly, so do not be afraid to bring a pashmina, scarf, or jacket.

Wedding Reception

  • Canyon Kitchen at Lonesome Valley
    94 Lonesome Valley Road
    Sapphire, North Carolina 28717
  • 5:30 PM to 11:00PM

The reception will be held just steps from the ceremony location at Canyon Kitchen at Lonesome Valley. Plan for a night of delicious food and embarrassing dance moves. Transportation will be provided after the reception to High Hampton and the Hampton Inn.

Attire:
What you wore to the ceremony + dancing shoes and party hat.
