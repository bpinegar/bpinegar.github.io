Travel

We know that our wedding will be a time & financial investment for many of you and we want to say thank you, in advance, for making the trip. We can't wait to see you in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains!

Airports

The closest airports to Cashiers are:

  • Asheville Regional Airport (AVL)
    ~1 hour, 15 minutes driving
  • Greenville-Spartanburg International Airport (GSP)
    ~1 hour, 45 minutes driving
  • Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL)
    ~2 hours, 45 minutes driving
  • Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT)
    ~3 hours driving

Rental Cars & Shuttles

For those guests that plan on flying in to the area, a rental car may make a lot of sense. Cashiers is somewhat remote, and hitchiking may take a while. Rental cars should be available at all of the airports.

If you are interested in arranging transport from one of the nearby airports, you can call RoadRunner Driving Service at 828-524-3265 or 706-201-7719, or Matt Long at 704-860-4515. Also, guests staying at High Hampton can arrange for airport pick-up and drop-off by calling 1-800-334-2551.

We will be providing transportation to and from the ceremony and reception from the accommodations listed below.

Accommodations

Fall is a popular time in Cashiers, so we strongly encourage you to make your lodging reservations as soon as possible (ideally several months in advance). We have arranged for room blocks at the following places. Please ask for the Weems-Pinegar party.

High Hampton Inn

Charming inn and resort offering golf, tennis, hiking, paddle boating, swimming and more. Located about 4 miles from Lonesome Valley.

Reservations need to be made over the phone. Rooms rate are $165/night for single occupancy, $225/night for double occupancy, plus $52/night per additional person. A resort fee and taxes also apply. Breakfast is included in rate.
