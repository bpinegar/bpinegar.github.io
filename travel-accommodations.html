Travel

We know that our wedding will be a time & financial investment for many of you and we want to say thank you, in advance, for making the trip. We can't wait to see you in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains!

Airports

The closest airports to Cashiers are:

  • Asheville Regional Airport (AVL): 1 hour, 15 minutes
  • Greenville-Spartanburg International Airport (GSP): 1 hour, 30 minutes
  • Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL): 2 hours, 30 minutes
  • Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT): 3 hours

Rental Cars & Shuttles

Rental cars are available at all airports. If you are interested in arranging transport from one of the nearby airports, you can call RoadRunner Driving Service at 828-524-3265 or 706-201-7719.

Throughout the wedding weekend, we will be providing transportation from the accommodations listed below.

Accommodations

Fall is a popular time in Cashiers, so we strongly encourage you to make your lodging reservations as soon as possible (ideally several months in advance). We have arranged for room blocks at the following places. Please ask for the Weems-Pinegar party.

Charming inn and resort offering golf, tennis, hiking, paddle boating, swimming and more. Located about 4 miles from Lonesome Valley.

Reservations need to be made over the phone. Rooms starting at $165/night for single occupancy. $225/night for double occupancy. $52/night per additional person. Breakfast is included in rate.
