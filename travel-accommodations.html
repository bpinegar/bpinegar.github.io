Travel

Airports

The closest airports to Cashiers are:

  • Asheville Regional Airport (AVL): 1 hour, 15 minutes
  • Greenville-Spartanburg International Airport (GSP): 1 hour, 30 minutes
  • Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL): 2 hours, 30 minutes
  • Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT): 3 hours

Rental Cars & Shuttles

Rental cars are available at all airports. If you are interested in arranging transport from one of the nearby airports, you can call RoadRunner Driving Service at 828-524-3265 or 706-201-7719.

Throughout the wedding weekend, we will be providing transportation from the accommodations listed below.
